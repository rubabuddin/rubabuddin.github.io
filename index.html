Hey, I'm Rubab!

I'm an engineer, fitness enthusiast, and avid traveller.

"When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor."

- Elon Musk

Take risks, make mistakes, and progress. That has always been my personal philosophy and has kept life nothing short of interesting.

I graduated from the Electrical & Computer Engineering program from the University of Toronto in June 2015. I obtained a double minor in Sustainable Energy and in Engineering Business (in collaboration with the Rotman School of Management).

Currently working as a Product Manager at ARnocular. Previously completed a 14-month engineering internship at Microsemi SoC in San Jose, California.

I'm passionate about evolving products and disruptive technologies. Always open to discussing new opportunities. Feel free to contact me.

Outside of engineering and technology, I like to travel, absorb different cultures and enjoy amazing food. I have always had a passion for sports and personal fitness - not only to lead a healthy lifestyle but to bring out my competitive spirit.
